Geometric Analysis meets Geometric Topology

25 - 28 February 2019, University of Heidelberg

About

The goal of the conference is to bring together researchers in Geometric Analysis and Geometric Topology. We will also have an informal panel discussions about academic job markets in Europe and a meeting and discussion with an expert on impostor syndrome.

This conference is funded by the Gender Equality funds of the SPP Geometry at Infinity and our speakers are all women. Everyone is welcome to participate.

We support the Statement of Inclusiveness.

Funding for young researchers

We have some limited funding for accomodation available for graduate students and postdocs. If you are interested in attending the conference and would like to apply for funding, please fill out the form below and attach a current CV. Funded students and postdocs will be accomodated in double rooms. Please state possible prefered roommates in the form below.

We cannot guarantee that we will be able to cover travel costs as well. Please apply for other sources as well.

Deadline for applications is December, 15th 2018. We will then evaluate all applications and inform you about the decisions in the beginning of 2019.
